# Piggys Variety Mod

This is a "fork" of the
original [Piggy's Variety Mod](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/Piggy/Piggys_Variety_Mod/).<br>
Add various vanilla style things, currently adds ~~2~~ 1 Custom weapon and 1 hazard.

## Features

### Tesla Gate

- Has a chance to spawn instead of a secure door.
  ![TeslaGate](https://i.imgur.com/hvGazNs.png)

### Rifle

- A fully automatic weapon boasting tremendous power. Extremely low chance to spawn on any moon.
- Requires "Magazine" item to fire. This also has a low chance of spawning.
- Press Q to check the remaining ammo in your rifle. Press R to reload (Changeable).
  ![Rifle](https://i.imgur.com/qPSkppi.gif)
